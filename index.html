What We Do


Minimizing waste, delivering resources

We help communities + businesses maintain value in their waste and channel it to biogas plants, composting facilities, and other recyclers who convert this high-quality waste into new products

Helping everyone do the right thing

Our mobile tools and online dashboards connect a community of residents, businesses, collectors, and processors and help them do their part in reducing landfills.

A SMART, EFFICIENT, ACCOUNTABLE WASTE-TO-RESOURCE NETWORK

Over 85% of India’s waste can be recycled yet 90% sits in landfills today. We preserve value of waste at source and connect communities, businesses, waste collectors, energy producers, and recyclers through transparent transactions. Join our network to create smarter, cleaner, healthier neighbourhoods and better civic services.

How Our Network Works

We help communities and businesses maintain value of their waste at source. Our collectors and processors turn this waste into new products. Together, we reduce landfills, power clean energy, and build recycling economies.
